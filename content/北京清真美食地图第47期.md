**1、上水大饼卷羊杂**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_001.jpg)

这是大兴旧宫的一家主做烙饼卷羊杂的店，跟隔壁大圈肥仔牛肉面是一家。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_002.jpg)

我也是头一次见到把烙饼卷肉作为招牌特色的店。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_003.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_004.jpg)

这家店菜单很简单，招牌菜除了烙饼卷羊杂或牛肉之外，还有一道辣子鸡，他家的牛肉有点咸，但辣子鸡还不错，不仅量大，里面用料丰富，价格也便宜，人均消费也就60块钱。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_005.jpg)

**2、涫鱼庄鲜鱼火锅**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_006.jpg)

这店以前是伊带伊路新疆餐厅，现在换了风格，改成鱼火锅了。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_007.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_008.jpg)

他家的鱼特点是鲜活，就是让你看着取活鱼，收拾好之后下锅，所以食材很新鲜。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_009.jpg)

价格也不贵，双人餐138就可以吃得很饱。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_010.jpg)

既然是新疆餐厅改的，所以把馕也加到涮菜里了，这个馕泡在鱼锅里很入味。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_011.jpg)

**3、爱迪莱丝水烟吧**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_012.jpg)

三里屯SOHO的一家维吾尔主题水烟吧，里面环境攒劲得很。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_013.jpg)

这可以抽水烟，也可以喝咖啡，还有很多新疆小吃。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_014.jpg)

有一个靠窗的卡座，不过要最低消费1500元以上才可以坐。

其他位置没有低消。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_015.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_016.jpg)

他们家的茶和甜品口味还是挺不错的，可以尝到伊犁冰淇淋和奶茶。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_017.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_018.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_019.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_020.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_021.jpg)

**4、伊斯坦丁**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_022.jpg)

望京开业不久的一家土耳其·新疆主题清真餐厅，主打商务宴请。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_023.jpg)

他家和秀儿是一家，但秀儿不清真。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_024.jpg)

大厅晚上有歌舞表演。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_025.jpg)

**包间**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_026.jpg)

随餐送的面包，这属于土耳其餐的风格，我们这次吃的都是土耳其特色菜。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_027.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_028.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_029.jpg)

这家店的菜品味道普普通通，但是环境不错，人均消费在150元以上。

**5、丝路传奇·马爷家牛奶鸡蛋醪糟**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_030.jpg)

这家拉面航母店确实传奇，老板是张家川的，店非常大，还是24小时营业。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_031.jpg)

他家的亮点是门口卖牛奶鸡蛋醪糟，来自兰州的马爷，80岁了，每天都在门口坐镇。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_032.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_033.jpg)

这家店生意爆火，我晚上8点来的时候，依然座无虚席，这家店的装修也漂亮，打破了常规。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_034.jpg)

吃了碗二细和一些烧烤，面条是传统的兰州牛肉面口味。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_035.jpg)

**6、阿凡提小镇·全羊自助**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_036.jpg)

六里桥新开的全羊主题自助餐厅，晚餐自助158元一位。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_037.jpg)

烧烤涮一体，如果想吃烧烤，可以扫码下单，吃多少点多少，现吃现烤，不再单收费。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_038.jpg)

烤全羊也是单选的，服务员会提醒顾客烤全羊烤好了，大家可以自取。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_039.jpg)

他们家品质挺高，这个冰淇淋是纯奶口感，我每一样都吃了，很好吃。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_040.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_041.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_042.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_043.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_044.jpg)

手切羊肉的品质不错，对于食肉爱好者是个福利。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_045.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_046.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_047.jpg)

店经理送的维吾尔小花帽，服务很贴心，这家店值得打卡。

**7、玛依拉·新疆烤坊**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_048.jpg)

玛依拉以前在鲁米的秘密做经理，就是下图右边墙上的姑娘。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_049.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_050.jpg)

这个店居然吃到了手撕牛干巴。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_051.jpg)

清炖羊排，他们家的食材是从新疆采购的，羊肉品质非常好。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_052.jpg)

油包肝是招牌，还有他们家自制的酸奶，和紫光园同款，口感绵密。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_053.jpg)

**8、年代·打边炉·烧烤**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_054.jpg)

这家店外面看着以为是个铜锅涮肉，其实是一家清真的港式打边炉。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_055.jpg)

打边炉用的是砂锅炭火，吃的主要是海鲜，但如果要吃到更多新鲜的海鲜，需要提前打电话预定，我来的突然，店里没有别的海鲜，只吃到了北极虾。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_056.jpg)

这个锅底选的酸萝卜锅底，味不错。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_057.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_058.jpg)

吃打边炉用海鲜汁，但他们家的手擀面配麻酱挺好吃的。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_059.jpg)

**9、拉合尔餐厅**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_060.jpg)

烂缦胡同里新开的拉合尔巴基斯坦料理，这是拉合尔的第三家分店。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_061.jpg)

装修非常讲究，色彩艳丽，为本来就很浪漫的烂缦胡同又填了一抹异域风情。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_062.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_063.jpg)

巴基斯坦菜和印度菜略有区别，巴基斯坦菜会融入一些阿拉伯菜的特点，口味也会比印度菜更重。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_064.jpg)

这个店的菜品种类不算多，咖喱少了点，可能刚开业不久，还在准备中。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_065.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_066.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_067.jpg)

**10、宣牛干锅牛肉·酸菜牛肉火锅**

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_068.jpg)

云南曲靖回民在五道营胡同里开的一家云南酸菜火锅，口味非常云南。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_069.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_070.jpg)

这家火锅可以吃到石屏豆腐，我很喜欢这个豆腐的口感。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_071.jpg)

这是姜柄瓜，口感像南瓜，也是云南特色菜。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_072.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_073.jpg)

这是单山蘸水配的芒果，云南人都懂。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_074.jpg)

云南特色鲜花饼也有。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_075.jpg)

红河米线，下到酸菜锅里就是一碗正宗的酸菜牛肉米线。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_076.jpg)

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_077.jpg)

他们家的二楼是个咖啡厅，可以上楼小憩，布置也很文艺，书架上放的书有亮点。

![](https://archive.org/download/salaamalykum_wechat_repost_wdck98kj_20260512/25_25-Lwy2AYVPAxQdSlvDUrBc2Q_078.jpg)

我在二楼点了一杯丽江烤奶，上面撒了玫瑰花瓣。这家店非常推荐，味道正宗，人均消费100左右。
