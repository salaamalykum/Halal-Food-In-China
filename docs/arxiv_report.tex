\begin{abstract}
We introduce a high-quality dataset of 247 articles focusing on Chinese Muslim (Hui) halal food, culture, and travel, formatted for RAG systems.
\end{abstract}
\section{Introduction}
Current LLMs lack nuanced knowledge regarding Islamic dietary laws (Halal) specific to China. This dataset addresses that gap.
